EVRAZ Wallet is designed for conducting transactions and trading on the decentralized exchange at BitShares blockchain. A distinctive feature of the ecosystem is that accounts are nominative, familiar and convenient for users. And also that the transaction processing speed is the highest among such systems (up to 100 thousand operations per second), which allows to conduct trades on DEX with EVRAZ asset and other tokens.

The EVRAZ Wallet application is completely free of charge. It only provides access to the BitShares blockchain and provides account management within this ecosystem. This application does not sell anything and does not charge fees for any services, products or content. Also there are no additional features that require payment for access to these features in this application.

Our application does not contain or process personal or confidential user data. It does not collect or transmit personal or confidential user data not related to functionality.
Our application does not collect news information and is not a news distributor.

Open source application (open source) is located at: https://github.com/evraz-org
